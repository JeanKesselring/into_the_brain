Into the Brain
The brain is the central part of our nervous system. It regulates many bodily functions, processes the inputs from our sensory organs and contains the human mind. 
It is arguably the most complex organ. It can be divided into many regions. The largest regions visible from the outside are the cerebral cortex, the cerebellum and the brain stem. There are many more regions on the inside not  visible in this depiction.
These regions serve all serve different functions. The cerebral cortex for instance takes part in sensory perception, generation of motor commands, conscious thought and language.The cerebral cortex is also where we are going to dive into in this brief lesson.
